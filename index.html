Score is made of two components: donation score and war participation score.

Donation Score:

You are expected to donate 6 cards per day, on average. If you make more, you will gain points. If you make fewer, you will lose points. If you have zero, you will be penalized -40 points

War Participation Score:

For each of the last 10 wars, did member...: Yes: No:
...participate in the war? +0 -1
...complete each collection battle? (per battle) +0 -5
...win each collection battle? (per battle) +2 +0
...complete war day battle? +15 -30
...win war day battle? (per battle) +5 +0
